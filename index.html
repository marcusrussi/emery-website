Emery Infrastructure

Emery provides data infrastructure consulting and contracting services for academic research. We are dedicated to helping academic teams produce high quality science quickly and efficiently thanks to stable and reliable data infrastructure. Our teams brings over 10 years of experience in both industry and academia and an intimate familiarity with both the technical needs of production quality data systems and the workflows and unique needs of academic research.

Consulting

We leverage our experience working across multiple teams and organizations in industry and academia to find the right data system solutions for your particular research needs. Whether you are interested in social media or geo-spatial data, machine learning or causal inference, in producing original research or supporting data services for multiple researchers we are happy to provide our unique perspective in choosing your technical stack, designing your data pipeline architecture, providing prototypes and proofs of concept, or making security and privacy recommendations via our consulting service.

Contracting

In addition to our consulting services we also offer software and data engineering contracting services. We are happy to work within or outside of your technical stack to build out or improve existing data systems. We offer short term project-based contracts as well as monthly maintenance retainer contracts for system support.
